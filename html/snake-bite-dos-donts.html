Snake Bite Do’s & Don’ts

Important first aid guidelines for snake bite emergencies

DO

  • Keep the patient calm and still
  • Take the patient to the nearest hospital immediately
  • Remove tight clothing, shoes, or jewelry
  • Keep the bitten limb at rest
  • Note the snake’s appearance if possible

DON’T

  • Do not cut, suck, or burn the wound
  • Do not apply tourniquet or tight band
  • Do not give alcohol or home remedies
  • Do not try to catch or kill the snake
  • Do not delay medical treatment
🚨 Emergency Snake Bite? Go to the nearest hospital immediately
