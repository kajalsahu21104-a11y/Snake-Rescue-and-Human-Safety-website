Why Snake Rescue Matters

SNAKE RESCUE CALL is the incredible true story about the dedicated wildlife conservationists who work to solve human-snake conflict in their communities while risking their own lives to protect some of India’s most dangerous snake species.

Each year, 2.7 million people suffer serious snakebites, and India alone accounts for over 46,000 deaths annually. Despite their reputation, snakes are vital for the ecosystem, controlling pests and maintaining food chain balance.
